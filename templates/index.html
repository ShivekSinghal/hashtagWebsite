Are the classes suitable for beginners?

Yes! All our classes are beginner-friendly. You don't need any prior dance experience—just the enthusiasm to learn and have fun.

We offer Jazz, Ballet, Modern Contemporary, Hip Hop, Heels, Kids Jazz, and Kids Ballet at our various studios across Delhi NCR.

Absolutely! We offer paid drop-in classes so you can experience the vibe before committing to a full batch.
