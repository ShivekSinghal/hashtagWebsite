Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Open Class?

An Open Class is a one-time, non-committal dance session designed for anyone who wants to explore a new style, meet our instructors, and experience the vibe of our academy.

Who can attend the Open Class?

Anyone! Whether you're a complete beginner, a dance enthusiast, or a returning student — our open classes are inclusive and beginner-friendly.

Do I need to register in advance?

Yes, pre-registration is required as spots are limited at each of our 8 studio locations. Just hit the “Register Now” button and choose your preferred studio.

What is the fee for the Open Class?

The Open Class is priced at 720/- per participant.

What styles will be offered in the Open Class?

Styles vary by studio, but you can expect classes in Jazz, Ballet, Modern Contemporary, Hip Hop and Heels. Full schedules are available during registration.

What should I wear or bring?

Wear comfortable clothes you can move in, and bring water and a small towel. Dance shoes or clean sneakers are recommended depending on the style.

Can I bring a friend?

Absolutely! Dancing with friends makes it more fun. Just make sure they register too.

Will this class help me decide on joining a regular batch?

Yes! That’s what it’s for. You’ll get a real taste of our teaching style, studio energy, and what to expect from our regular batches.

Where are your studios located?

We have 8 locations across Delhi NCR — Noida (Sector 43 & 50), Indirapuram, Gurgaon, South Delhi, Rajouri Garden, East Delhi & Pitampura.

Who will be teaching the Open Class?

Our certified and experienced faculty — the same amazing artists who teach our regular batches — will be leading these sessions.
